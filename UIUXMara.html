Examples of User story and User Case

User story

User Purpose: Purchase of chocolate products as a snack.
Objectives: Selecting and purchasing chocolate products with natural composition for themselves and their loved ones.
Requirements for the online store: Filtering by product composition and ingredients, reviews and ratings, description of the composition of each product.

User Case

Scenario title: Reviewing a product review.
Actor: Maria, 42 years old, works in finance, leads a healthy lifestyle.
Purpose: To view a review of a selected product.
Prerequisites: Maria has access to the internet.
Main Sequence: 1. Maria opens an Internet browser and goes to the page of the online store where the specific product whose review she wants to read is located.
2. Maria scrolls down the page to the customer reviews section.
3. Maria reads the reviews, starting with the most recent or most relevant ones. She pays attention to the positive and negative aspects mentioned in the reviews.
4. Maria analyzes the content of the reviews and draws conclusions about the quality and usefulness of the product.
5. If Maria has questions or wants more information, she can use the comments function or contact information to get in touch with other buyers or customer service.
6. After reading the reviews, Maria makes an informed decision to purchase the product based on the information and her preferences.
Alternate sequence of steps:
1. Maria opens an Internet browser and goes to the page of the online store where the specific product whose review she wants to read is located.
2. Maria scrolls down the page to the customer reviews section.
3. Instead of reading the reviews right away, Maria first pays attention to the overall rating of the product and the number of reviews. She looks at high scores and a large number of reviews as indicators of the quality of the product.
4. Maria looks at the few reviews with the highest ratings and most positive comments. She looks for confirmation of her expectations of the product.
5. If Maria has questions or wants more information, she can look at the comments on the reviews or ask her question in the comments section.
6. After reading several reviews, Maria draws conclusions about the product and makes an informed decision to purchase it.
