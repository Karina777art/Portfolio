About project

The brand concept is a combination of rose and ice. The rose acts as a symbol of youth and ice as an attribute that keeps youth eternal by freezing it. The resulting product should be visually appealing, yet not overly detailed or cluttered. The brend name is “Isro”. I - ice and is, ro - rose.
